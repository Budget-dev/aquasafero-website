# **App Name**: Vael Studio

## Core Features:

- Cinematic Work Showcase: An interactive, high-contrast grid to display high-resolution film projects with smooth transition effects on hover.
- Vision AI Treatment Tool: An AI assistant used as a tool to generate professional cinematic visual treatments and atmosphere descriptions from brief story premises.
- Kinetic Scroll Work Reel: A horizontal auto-scrolling work reel that serves as a visual background for exploring cinematic sequences and photography highlights.
- Accolade Tracker: A refined, list-based display of festival wins, awards, and recognitions with distinct classification labels for categories like Feature and Short.
- Script Concept Generator: A generative tool powered by AI that provides unique storytelling hooks and scene directions based on lighting styles or chosen film genres.
- Director’s Narrative Portal: A minimal contact and representation interface allowing for cinematic treatment inquiries through an elegant, typography-focused form.
- Media Content Management: A specialized media manager for maintaining the 'Behind the Frame' photo gallery and project metadata using a remote storage backend.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Burnt Amber (#C16C27) symbolizing the warmth of 35mm film stock and low-light embers.
- Background color: Obsidian Dark (#0D0B0A), a desaturated variation of the primary hue providing cinematic depth.
- Accent color: Deep Ochre (#997732) to highlight calls-to-action and festival awards with a subtle analogous contrast.
- Font pairing: 'Literata' for a high-contrast literary serif feel in headlines, paired with 'Inter' as a machined, neutral sans-serif for project metadata and specifications.
- Minimalist, thin-line vector icons inspired by lens focus points and camera viewfinder framing.
- A vast, asymmetric layout utilizing significant whitespace (void) and editorial-style image positioning to mirror wide cinematic aspect ratios.
- Smooth, gravity-based kinetic scrolling and magnetic button interactions to evoke the weighted feel of physical cinema equipment.
